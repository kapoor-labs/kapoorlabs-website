# Agent Alina — AI Travel Chatbot

## Overview

Agent Alina was an AI-based travel assistant designed to help customers find better flight options through conversational interaction and automated search workflows.

## Problem

Travel customers often compare many dates, airports, prices, and itinerary combinations before booking. This creates repeated manual searches and decision fatigue.

## Solution

Agent Alina combined chatbot interaction with automated background exploration of alternate travel options. Instead of forcing customers to repeat searches manually, the bot could proactively explore nearby dates and alternatives.

## Reported Experiment Outcomes

- Customers who interacted with the chatbot were about 20% more likely to complete a booking.
- Users performed about 30% fewer manual searches because the bot explored alternate options in the background.
