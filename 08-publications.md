# Research & Publications

## Efficient Storage and Retrieval of In-Memory Static Data

**Author:** Anuj Kapoor  
**Document Type:** Research Paper  
**Journal:** Journal of Algorithms and Computation  
**Volume / Issue:** Volume 52, Issue 1  
**Pages:** 83–96  
**Publication Date:** June 2020  
**DOI:** 10.22059/jac.2020.76227

[Read the paper](https://jac.ut.ac.ir/article_76227_f4783d3e9e2ddaf29259318978af6743.pdf)

## Abstract Summary

The paper discusses limitations of traditional hash-based and B-tree-based composite indexes for in-memory search over semi-static data. It proposes an algorithmic technique and supporting data structures to enable flexible search by possible key combinations without requiring composite indexes for every combination.
