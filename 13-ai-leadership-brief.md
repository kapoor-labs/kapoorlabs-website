# AI Leadership Brief

## Executive Summary

I build and scale AI products that solve real customer problems. My leadership style combines product clarity, engineering depth, and execution discipline so teams can ship fast without sacrificing trust, reliability, or long-term maintainability.

## Where I Add the Most Value

- AI support and productivity platforms
- Copilot and agent-based workflow systems
- Enterprise automation with measurable customer and business impact
- Product areas where fast experimentation and robust architecture must co-exist

## Leadership Operating Model

### 1. Strategy to Execution
- Translate ambiguous customer pain into clear product bets.
- Define technical strategy, architecture, and delivery phases.
- Prioritize outcomes over feature volume.

### 2. Build High-Performance Teams
- Coach engineers to think in product outcomes, not just tickets.
- Establish strong design and code quality standards.
- Create ownership culture across product, engineering, and operations.

### 3. AI Quality and Trust
- Put prompt and response evaluation loops into the development lifecycle.
- Treat safety, reliability, and observability as first-class requirements.
- Use iterative measurement to improve model-assisted workflows.

## Selected Evidence of Impact

- Agent Alina showed approximately 20% higher booking likelihood among users who engaged with the chatbot.
- Agent Alina users performed approximately 30% fewer manual searches due to proactive alternate-option exploration.
- Built and co-developed AI-assisted support workflows focused on improving support speed, quality, and consistency.

## 30-60-90 Day Leadership Plan (Typical)

### First 30 Days
- Understand customer pain, funnel bottlenecks, and current architecture.
- Align stakeholders on success metrics and constraints.

### Days 31-60
- Launch focused pilot improvements in high-impact workflows.
- Establish a quality loop for prompts, grounding, and response outcomes.

### Days 61-90
- Scale validated patterns into platform capabilities.
- Formalize roadmap, team ownership model, and execution cadence.

## Roles I Am Best Aligned With

- Head of AI Product Engineering
- Director of AI Engineering
- Principal AI Product and Platform Lead
