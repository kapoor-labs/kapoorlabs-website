# Alexa Smart Home Registrations

## Overview

At Amazon, I worked on Alexa Smart Home customer-facing experiences that helped users set up and manage smart home devices more successfully.

## Problem

Smart Home setup can be complex for customers because it involves devices, accounts, skills, network state, partner integrations, and multiple points of possible failure. A poor setup experience can reduce customer confidence and prevent successful device activation.

## Solution

The work focused on improving customer-facing onboarding and registration experiences so customers could more reliably connect and manage Smart Home devices through Alexa.

## My Contribution

I contributed as a Senior Full Stack Engineer, working on implementation, customer-facing flows, service reliability, and automation features for Alexa Smart Home device setup.
