I am an AI-focused software engineer and product-minded builder with 16 years of experience delivering customer-facing platforms, scalable cloud systems, and intelligent automation experiences.

I currently work as a Senior Software Engineer at Microsoft, where my focus is on AI-powered customer support, Copilot experiences, agent integrations, tool orchestration, and productivity workflows for support engineers.

I describe my work through three themes:

### 1. Creative AI Builder
I enjoy identifying customer friction and converting it into AI-first product experiences. I have worked on AI support platforms, chatbot experiences, intelligent recommendations, semantic search, prompt strategy, evaluation loops, and agent orchestration.

### 2. Customer-Obsessed Engineer
Much of my work has focused on measurable customer impact: faster case resolution, easier device onboarding, better travel discovery, and reduced manual effort in complex workflows.

My engineering background includes cloud platforms, microservices, databases, search systems, in-memory data structures, customer-facing experimentation, and enterprise-scale automation.
