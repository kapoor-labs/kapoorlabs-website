# Copilot for Customer Support

## Overview

Copilot for Customer Support is an AI-powered productivity experience for support engineers. The goal is to help frontline support teams move faster from customer pain to meaningful action by bringing case intelligence, recommended next steps, guided prompts, and agent integrations into a single workflow.

## Problem

Customer support workflows are often fragmented across case notes, emails, knowledge articles, historical cases, diagnostics, and internal tools. Engineers may spend significant time reconstructing context before they can take effective action.

## Solution

The system brings AI-powered support capabilities into the engineer workflow, helping users:

- Understand case context quickly
- Generate handover summaries
- Identify recommended next actions
- Ask case-specific questions
- Navigate complex case history
- Trigger specialized agents and tools
- Improve consistency in customer communication
